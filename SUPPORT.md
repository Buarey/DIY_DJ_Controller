# Support

For build help, open an issue and include:

- Your operating system.
- Mixxx or Rekordbox version.
- Board model and USB connector type.
- Photos of the wiring or PCB area involved.
- The exact firmware commit or release you used.
- What you expected, what happened, and what you already tried.

For quick reports, use the issue templates. For larger design ideas, open a feature request before starting a big pull request.
