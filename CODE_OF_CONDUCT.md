# Code Of Conduct

This project should be welcoming to people learning electronics, DJ software, firmware, CAD, and open hardware. Be patient with beginner questions and practical build failures; that is where useful documentation comes from.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when someone is debugging a physical build.
- Keep criticism specific to the work.
- Share enough detail that others can reproduce your results.
- Credit other projects, designs, and contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Gatekeeping based on skill level, tools, budget, location, or background.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Misrepresenting another person's work as your own.
- Repeatedly pushing unrelated arguments after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull requests; or block contributors who make the project hostile or unsafe. If there is a problem, open an issue with the relevant context or contact the maintainer privately if public discussion would make things worse.
